The Enterprise High School Career and Technology Center TSA chapter is one of the best in Alabama, and is proud to represent TSA and all it stands for, acting as a beacon to all who wish to forward their education and understanding of technology. Our web development team is a group of enthusiastic Sophmores that are passionate about creating digital solutions. Using our web development skills, we build user-friendly websites to teach users about the benfits of worthy causes. We are committed to learning and using the latest technologies to support TSA's efforts and make a positive impact in our community.
