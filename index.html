Featured Initiatives



American Clean Power Association (ACP)

The American Clean Power Association (ACP) is a national trade association representing companies in
the renewable energy sector, including wind, solar, storage, and transmission companies. The ACP
advocates for policies that promote the growth of clean energy industries and works to remove barriers
to renewable energy development. They also provide industry research, resources, and networking
opportunities to their members to support the advancement of clean energy technologies.

U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Water Power Technologies Office

The U.S. Department of Energy's Water Power Technologies Office (WPTO) supports research, development,
and deployment of hydroelectric and marine energy technologies. They fund innovative projects,
provide technical assistance, and collaborate with industry partners to drive advancements in water
power technologies, including conventional hydropower, pumped storage, and marine energy.
