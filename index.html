Call to Action

Welcome to the Wildcat Green Energy initiative. Our planet is currently grappling with the adverse effects of global warming, a result of pollutants like carbon dioxide trapping solar radiation and exacerbating the greenhouse effect. Despite these challenges, we believe in the power of every individual to combat this crisis by embracing renewable energy and reducing emissions. Wildcat Energy is dedicated to assisting you in achieving sustainable solutions. One of the most impactful actions individuals can take is to create eco-friendly homes. Through various measures such as solar panels, energy conservation, hydroelectric energy, and wind energy, homeowners can contribute significantly to a cleaner environment. Explore our website to discover green energy solutions, financial incentives, and sustainable practices for your home, and take the first step towards a cleaner future today!


Featured Initiatives

  • American Clean Power Association (ACP)

  • The American Clean Power Association (ACP) is a national trade association representing companies in the renewable energy sector, including wind, solar, storage, and transmission companies. The ACP advocates for policies that promote the growth of clean energy industries and works to remove barriers to renewable energy development. They also provide industry research, resources, and networking opportunities to their members to support the advancement of clean energy technologies.

  • SunPower Corporation



  • SunPower Corp. is a leading solar energy company that designs and manufactures high-efficiency solar panels and solar systems for residential, commercial, and utility-scale applications. They offer complete solar solutions, including installation, monitoring, and maintenance services, and are known for their innovative solar technology and commitment to sustainability.

  • U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Water Power Technologies Office

  • The U.S. Department of Energy's Water Power Technologies Office (WPTO) supports research, development, and deployment of hydroelectric and marine energy technologies. They fund innovative projects, provide technical assistance, and collaborate with industry partners to drive advancements in water power technologies, including conventional hydropower, pumped storage, and marine energy.
